About Me
Hello, my name is Veena Walker and I specialize in teaching computers and have experience in teaching html/css to high schoolers. Currently I teach coding to elementary students we use Bitsbox where we make apps. Additionally, the 5th graders are learning html using Khan Academy and we use the Hour of Code website.

I hold a Bachelor of Technology Education from Eastern Michigan University and a Master’s of Educational Leadership. Currently, I am at the University of Denver for a Coding Bootcamp and looking forward to an amazing journey towards a career change.
